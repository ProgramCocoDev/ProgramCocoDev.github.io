Seeking Light

Steam The GDWC 2022 Winner

Published in 2022

As a member of the Lucky Dodo team, I had the pleasure of working with a great team on Seeking Light. The game combines Souls-like mechanics with Metroidvania exploration, and I was responsible for several key systems throughout its development.

Core Systems

  • Event Dialogue System - Used both to explain the story of the game and the interactions between Sunny and the bosses
  • Game flow and progression mechanics
  • Save system implementation
  • Level streaming optimization
  • UI/UX design and implementation
  • Visual effects and particle systems

Technical Features

  • Souls-like teleport and rest point system
  • AI behavior system (early development)
  • Level streaming for seamless world exploration
  • Save system with checkpoint management
  • Visual effects and environmental interactions

The game's success is reflected in its achievements, including winning the GDWC 2022 Hobby Category Award and receiving other nominations. The project is published on Steam and showcases our team's ability to create an engaging gaming experience with innovative systems and polished mechanics.

About Me

My journey in game development began with a simple question: "How did they do that?" This curiosity was sparked while playing World of Warcraft, where I found myself captivated by the intricate boss battles, innovative mechanics, and strategic gameplay. This passion evolved to encompass Souls-like games and Metroidvanias, genres that exemplify masterful game design and technical execution.

As a developer, I approach games with an analytical mindset, constantly studying mechanics, NPC behaviors, and narrative delivery. I have a particular interest in procedural generation, as it creates dynamic and engaging gameplay experiences through algorithmic design. My technical expertise extends to tool development and shader programming, where I combine mathematical principles with creative solutions.

My gaming background is deeply rooted in classic PlayStation titles like Crash Bandicoot and Spyro, which laid the foundation for my appreciation of game design. This evolved through experiences with iconic series such as Ratchet & Clank, Jak & Daxter, and modern masterpieces like Dark Souls. Each game has contributed to my understanding of what makes interactive entertainment compelling.

Beyond game development, I maintain a diverse set of interests including cinema, manga/anime, sports, and literature. These varied pursuits inform my creative process and help me approach challenges from multiple perspectives. I thrive on continuous learning and am always seeking new opportunities to expand my skills and knowledge.
