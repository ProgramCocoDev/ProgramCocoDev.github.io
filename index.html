Published on 2022.

As a member of the Lucky Dodo I had the pleasure of working with a great team on Seeking Light. I was responsible for the AI in the early stages and later the game flow, save systems, level streaming, UI and VFX. The game is published on Steam and won the GDWC 2022 Hobby Category Award and other nominations. Highlight the "Event Dialogue System" used both to explain the story of the game and the interactions between Sunny and the bosses. Other elements I worked on were a system of Teleports and Rest points similar to souls-like, the game saving, the level streaming and at the beginning of the project a bit of AI.
